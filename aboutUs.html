About Us


Arngren Electronics is a company that sells electronics, gadgets and post office mail items.

The company was started in 1983 by Frithjof Arngren. In 1994 they started with mail order sales, and were a pioneer in the field.

Our company has over 130,000 names in the customer list. We have received significant criticism for the design of their websites.

Frithjof Arngren

CEO & Founder

He started Arngren Electronics in 1983, and was a pioneer in mail order sales of electronics in Norway.

For a number of years he sold electronics and gadgets via mail order and shop and had a customer database of over 130,000 names. In January 2000, he sold the company to investor and entrepreneur Simon Yuen, who turned the internet sales business around.
